FAQs




How do I access nutrition information? If dining in our restaurant, please ask your server for nutrition information. If ordering online, please use our Contact Us page.
 
 
Where do you deliver? We deliver to addresses within a 10 mile radius of any of our locations.
 
 
Does Paolo's cater? Yes! Please use our Contact Us page for catering requests.
 
 
Is Paolo's allergy-friendly? We take food allergies very seriously. Please let your server know about any allergies if you dine in-restaurant. If ordering, please use our Contact Us page to add a note to your order.
 
 
Can I host a party at Paolo's? Yes! Please use our Contact Us page to notify us of a party request. We will be in touch to discuss event details. *Note: party requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance of event date.*
 
 
Is Paolo's hiring? For hiring inquiries, please do not use our Contact Us page. Please visit a Paolo's location. Our staff will be happy to give you job opening and hiring process information.
